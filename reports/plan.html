Idea

For my final project, I would like to make a web app that will crowd source and compile Covid-19 vaccine availability in NC and update data from the North Carolina Department of Health and Human Service (NCDHHS)'s database of vaccine locations (on the NCDHHS MySpot.NC.gov website). Though the NC.gov site provides functionality to search for where to get the vaccine but it does not show any data about vaccine availability at each location. Pharmacies and other clinics may update their availability frequently and as the demand for vaccines is so high right now, it is crucial to have up to date information. In hearing about some friends/family's difficulty in finding accurate and up to date vaccine availability back home in a different state, I hope that this website that aggregates vaccines appointments across the state and provides that info to users can help people save time and reduce barriers to getting vaccinated!

Scope

My goal is for the site to display up to date information about vaccine availability in NC. This will require features such as:

  • Locations and the number of available appointments
  • A map and ability to search, filter (by distance, city, county, etc.)
  • Interacivity in a map that can zoom in/out, search features, and expanding on location to see more details (such as a link to book).

I will consider using the Vue framework and some basic componnts. I will also need some sort of map API and will look into Google Maps or the Leaflet API.

Data Sources

Data may be hard to obtain as currently there are no open source/available complete datasets of vaccine distributors or availability (at least none that I know of). Currently, I am thinking of web scraping or at least compiling a list of providers from the Myspot.nc.gov site into a JSON file and then going into each individual provider site to further scrape and update data.
